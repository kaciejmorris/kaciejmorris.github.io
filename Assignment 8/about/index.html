Kacie Morris

Kacie Morris - JNL 211

I am a second year student at Syracuse's Newhouse School of Public Communications studying magazine, news, and digital journalism. I am on the magazine track and part of the fashion and beauty communications milestone.

In the future, I hope to write for a magazine or work in similar communicative fields such as social media, advertising, and more.

In high school, I worked for my school paper, The North Star, as a staff writer. Presently, I am a part of the Fetch Collective Magazine at Syracuse University where I am the fashion editor, as well as a staff writer for The Good Life Magazine.

Outside of school, I work for The Naperville Fertility Center, where I have been the social media manager and content creator since 2023.

When I am not in school, I enjoy dancing, spending time with family and friends, and traveling. I am also a second year member of the Syracuse chapter of the Alpha Epsilon Phi sorority.
